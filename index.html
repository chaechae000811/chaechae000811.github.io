COVID-19 LATEST UPDATE: ALL PERFORMANCES POSTPONED

The producers of Harry Potter and the Cursed Child and the Lyric Theater are committed to the safety and peace of mind of all audiences, cast and company and theatre staff. We have been closely monitoring the ongoing coronavirus (Covid-19) situation and as such, all performances of Harry Potter and the Cursed Child are postponed with return performances to be scheduled at a later date.

All patrons holding tickets will be contacted by their point of purchase regarding refund and exchange policies. For now, we would like to recognize our staff, artists and producers for their ongoing support. And especially you, for your continued commitment to the theatre community during this extremely difficult period. We look forward to welcoming you at Harry Potter and the Cursed Child soon where the magic continues on stage!

If you Purchased your tickets online: You will be contacted directly via email with the option to keep your tickets for a future performance or receive a full refund.
If you purchased tickets as part of a Group: Please contact your Group Sales Agent for more information regarding exchanges or refunds. Please retain your original tickets until you speak with the Group Sales Agent. If you purchased tickets through any other licensed sales channel (Broadway.com, TodayTix, etc.): Please return to the original point of purchase for more information regarding exchanges and refunds. If you have your tickets in hand, please retain them for a refund or exchange. For any other inquiries, please call 855-551-9777.
